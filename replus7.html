About Catalyst

Cross-border investment and facilitation platform orchestrating strategic joint ventures between US technology firms and Indian industrial partners

Catalyst Investment Fund specializes in facilitating strategic joint ventures between innovative US technology companies and established Indian manufacturing and public sector partners. We're positioned to capitalize on India's anticipated industrial surge from $4T to $10T GDP.

Unlike traditional VCs, we combine market entry guidance, regulatory navigation, and manufacturing scale-up expertise with deep local connectivity. Our focus is on hardware and manufacturing-integrated technology partnerships—matching world-class US innovation with India's production capabilities and distribution networks.

We target US-based technology companies that are hardware-centric, IP-driven, and scale-up ready, but lack existing India operations. Our value proposition centers on partnership and capacity building rather than pure capital injection.

With operations centered in Kolkata & Delhi and strong nationwide manufacturing/PSU connections, we facilitate entry across India and Southeast Asia through cost-effective manufacturing partnerships and government contract access.

  • Established manufacturing network

Strategic Focus Areas

We concentrate on hardware-intensive sectors where US technology innovation meets India's manufacturing excellence and export potential, with particular emphasis on renewable energy solutions.
  • 01 Energy & Green Tech (Primary Focus)

    Inverters, Battery Management Systems (BMS), Solar Racking & Tracking systems, Heat Transfer, Hydrogen Catalysts, Microgrid & Smart Grid Infrastructure, and more. Our interests remain wide as we leverage India's position as a global renewable energy manufacturing hub and export base, providing access to cost-effective production and Asia-wide distribution channels.

  • 02 Defense & Security

    Tactical drones, perimeter security systems, anti-intrusion technology, and secure optoelectronics. Strong connections with defense procurement officials and access to government contract opportunities through established PSU relationships.

  • 03 Healthcare Technology

    Diagnostics equipment, remote monitoring devices, medical device manufacturing, and telemedicine hardware. Access to India's growing healthcare infrastructure and export manufacturing capabilities.

  • 04 Manufacturing Technology

    Industrial fintech solutions, AI/ML for manufacturing optimization, and cybersecurity for operational technology. Leveraging India's digital transformation in manufacturing and Industry 4.0 adoption.
