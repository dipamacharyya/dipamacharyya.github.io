About Catalyst

Cross-border venture platform specialized in launching and scaling North American technology companies across Asian markets

Catalyst is a cross-border venture platform specialized in launching and scaling marketing for US technology companies in the Indian and broader Asian market. We focus on creating strategic marketing partnerships, including sales/distribution JVs, to unlock growth across defense, energy, healthcare, and industrial sectors.

Government connections and understanding of strategic programs enable fast-track market access while cutting through regulatory hurdles. We prioritize go-to-market strategies over manufacturing, helping companies establish sales channels and customer relationships first.

Our framework enables rapid market entry through established regulatory relationships, proof of concept deployment in weeks, and complete market penetration strategies tailored to each sector's unique requirements.

North American technology innovators face significant barriers when entering Asian markets - complex regulatory environments, unfamiliar buyer networks, and lengthy approval processes.

Catalyst bridges this gap through direct access to public sector and strategic buyers, streamlined by our regulatory connections and government-sponsored programs like ADITI for defense/security adoption and Make in India initiatives.

Our unique value proposition focuses on market entry and customer acquisition first, with optional manufacturing scale-up available for companies experiencing overwhelming demand.

Sector Focus

We specialize in four key sectors where our government connections and market knowledge provide maximum advantage for North American technology companies.
  • 01 Energy & Green Tech

    Solar racking and tracking systems, inverters, controller systems, heat transfer, smart grid, microgrid controls, energy storage solutions, hydrogen catalysts with emphasis on distributed sales and pilot programs through established utility partnerships.

  • 02 Defense & Security

    Drones, optoelectronic surveillance, anti-intrusion systems, and government contract routes. Leveraging ADITI Scheme and direct access to defense procurement networks.

  • 03 Healthcare Technology

    Devices for diagnostics, remote care, and telemedicine with established channel partners across hospitals, clinics, and healthcare networks.

  • 04 Industrial Technology

    Automation, predictive ML, OT/Cybersecurity solutions leveraging industrial buyers and Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs) for rapid market penetration.
