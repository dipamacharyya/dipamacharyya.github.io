Two Nondilutive Investment Models

Choose the partnership model that best fits your market entry and scaling objectives

Exclusive Licensing

We license your technology for India/SEA markets, handle all manufacturing, sales, and support while you retain IP ownership and receive guaranteed royalties.

  • Zero dilution or upfront investment
  • Guaranteed minimum royalties
  • Complete market responsibility

  • Performance milestones

Joint venture structure where we provide manufacturing infrastructure, working capital, and market access while sharing revenues and maintaining shared control.

  • Shared revenue model
